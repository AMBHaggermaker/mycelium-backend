Build two connected features: Business Pages and Professional Board on user profiles.
PART ONE — BUSINESS PAGES
Add a new Businesses section accessible from the Commons navigation. Create a businesses table with fields: id, owner_id (references users), business_name, business_type (independently_owned, locally_owned_franchise, cooperative, nonprofit, sole_proprietor), category (construction, retail, food_beverage, healthcare, legal, creative, trades, technology, childcare, education, agriculture, other), description, location_label, service_area, hours jsonb, contact_phone, contact_email, contact_preference (platform_message, phone, email), website_url, is_verified_local boolean default false, is_active boolean default true, created_at. Add a business_photos table: id, business_id, url, caption, is_cover boolean, created_at. Add a business_services table: id, business_id, name, description, price_range, created_at. Add backend routes: GET /api/businesses (list all active businesses with filters for category and type), POST /api/businesses (create — auth required, verified members only), GET /api/businesses/:id (full business profile with photos, services, recommendations, Hotlight posts), PATCH /api/businesses/:id (edit — owner only), DELETE /api/businesses/:id (deactivate — owner or admin only). Add business recommendations using the existing threads system — a thread scoped to the business. Business recommendations cannot be deleted by the business owner, only by admins for policy violations. The business owner can reply to recommendations. Add admin ability to grant verified_local badge to businesses after review — a Verify Business button in the admin panel Businesses tab. Local Commerce posts can optionally be linked to a business page — add an optional business_id field to posts so business posts appear on their business page automatically.
Build the Businesses frontend: A Business Directory page at /businesses showing all active businesses in a card grid with business name, type badge, category, location, cover photo, and a verified local badge if granted. Add search by name and filter by category and business type. Add a Create Business button for verified members. Build a full Business Profile page at /businesses/:id showing: cover photo banner, business name, owner name linked to their personal profile (always visible — cannot be hidden), business type badge, verified local badge if granted, category, location and service area, hours, contact options based on owner preference, about/description section, services and products list with price ranges, photo gallery using R2 storage, community recommendations thread (verified members only can post recommendations, one recommendation per member per business, owner can respond but not delete), Hotlight posts from this business in a tab, a Report Business button for policy violations. Add a My Businesses tab to the user profile page showing businesses they own. Add a business_id field to the post creation form in Local Commerce — optional dropdown showing their businesses so posts link back to the business page.
PART TWO — PROFESSIONAL BOARD ON PROFILES
Add a Professional board to the profile page board grid. This is one of the draggable boards. The Professional board contains: current occupation or role (text field), skills (tag input — type a skill and press enter to add, displayed as a tag cloud), services offered (links automatically to their Local Commerce posts tagged as services), business affiliations (links to their Business Pages — shows business name, type badge, their role at the business), portfolio (photos and links — uses R2 for photo uploads), availability status (available for work, not taking new clients, open to opportunities, not applicable), professional bio (separate from personal bio, focused on work and skills). Add skill endorsements — verified members can endorse specific skills on another member's Professional board. One endorsement per person per skill. Endorsements show as a count next to the skill tag with avatars of endorsers on hover. Cannot be self-endorsed. Add a GET /api/profiles/:username/professional route returning professional board data. Add PATCH /api/profiles/professional for updating professional info. Store professional data in a user_professional_profiles table with fields: id, user_id, occupation, skills text array, availability, professional_bio, portfolio_urls jsonb, created_at, updated_at. Add a skill_endorsements table: id, endorser_id, endorsed_id, skill, created_at, unique constraint on endorser_id plus endorsed_id plus skill.

On the Business Page show the owner's professional skills from their Professional Board. On the Personal Profile Professional Board show links to all their Business Pages. In the Local Commerce feed show a small business page link on posts that are linked to a business. In the Business Directory show a Recently Recommended section on the main page showing businesses that received recommendations in the last 30 days. Add businesses to the Live Network sidebar activity feed — new business created and new recommendation posted both appear as activity events.
